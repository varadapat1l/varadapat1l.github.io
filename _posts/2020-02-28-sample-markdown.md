I am a recent graduate from Michigan State University, with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Indian and South Asian Studies. 

My goal is to work as a data analyst to solve complex societal challenges. I believe data-driven research and analysis can lead to informed decisions that improve communities and enact long-term, positive change.

I am planning on further developing my skillset through the University of Michigan Masters in Applied Data Science program beginning Fall 2026. I hope to build upon the foundations of statistics, machine learning, and large-scale data analysis that I learned at MSU in order to work with complex datasets, build predictive and exploratory models, design effective solutions, and more. I hope to use these technical skills, critical thinking, and ethical perspectives necessary to use data science effectively in advancing society and reducing inequality.
